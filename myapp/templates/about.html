OUR STORY


About the Owner of Z Square Water Tech Solutions The inspiring journey of the owner of Z Square Water Tech Solutions began in 2018, with a humble start in the domestic RO service sector. At that time, he was the sole reseller of Hytech Company, tirelessly working by himself to establish a foothold in the market.

His dedication and perseverance paid off when, on New Year's Day in 2019, he secured his first commercial orderâ€”a pivotal moment that marked the beginning of significant growth. By the end of 2019, his relentless efforts had resulted in a client base of over 40+ commercial clients.

Even during the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic, he demonstrated exceptional commitment by obtaining government permission to continue providing essential services to his domestic clients.

This period of adversity showcased his resilience and ability to adapt, as he expanded the range of services to include pressure pumps, heat pumps, ionisers, and more.

Today, Z Square Water Tech Solutions boasts an impressive client base of over 200+ commercial and 5000+ domestic clients, spanning both sales and services. His team has grown to include more than 20+ skilled technicians, providing top-notch service across Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat, and Karnataka.

His dedication to excellence has not gone unnoticed; he has been awarded by both Lexipure and the Rotary Club of Udaipur for outstanding service.

OUR MISSION

At Zsquare Watertech, our mission is to ensure everyone has access to clean, safe, pure, and healthy drinking water. We believe every individual deserves water free from impurities and contaminants, and we are dedicated to making this a reality with our high-quality water purifiers.


We are committed to:
  • Excellence in Customer Service: Delivering unparalleled service to meet and exceed customer expectations.
  • Innovation: Developing advanced and effective water purification solutions.
  • Affordability: Providing cost-effective products that cater to the diverse needs of our customers.
  • Sustainability: Promoting eco-friendly practices and minimizing our environmental impact.

OUR VISION

At Zsquare Watertech, our vision is to be the leading provider of water purification solutions, recognized for our exceptional products, outstanding customer service, and commitment to sustainability.


Our vision includes:
  • Empowering Customers: Ensuring access to clean and safe drinking water, positively impacting health and well-being.
  • Building Relationships: Fostering long-term relationships with our customers, suppliers, and partners based on trust, reliability, and mutual respect.
